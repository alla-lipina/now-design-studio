Hello

Now is a multidisciplinary design studio specialized in graphic & web design, brand identity, art direction.

We are experienced across cultural sector, fashion, advertising and varied domains of business. Our strong background covers a wide range of skills in printing, typography, packaging, web design, exhibition and retail windows design and more.

Our approach is in seamlessly moving between the world cultural heritage of the past in arts, graphic design, typography and insightful look to the future. Synthesizing the past and the future in our design process we have the clear solution that works Now.

We value exciting, intelligent and simple solutions and love brave and daring experiments in different fields. Whether it’s a small start-up or a large business we have over 10 years experience and a wide trusted network of collaborators to create effective result.
